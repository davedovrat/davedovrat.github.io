Scrum

What is scrum?

  • Something to do with rugby, a metaphor for teamwork I guess.
  • A rigid set of practices, methods and rules that promote agility, when done right.
  • A rigid set of practices, methods and rules that kill agility, also when done right.

The Takeaway

Prioritize individuals and interactions over processes and tools

  • Use the tools available to enter an Agile state of mind.
  • Remember that your team is made of individuals with different backgrounds, skill-sets and characters. Work with that and not against that.
  • Discard the tools that don't fit your team.

Working software over comprehensive documentation

  • TDD where applicable.
  • Short HLD.
  • Prioritize features over wikis.

Customer collaboration over contract negotiation

  • Short, frequent sprints.
  • MVP hop.
  • Don't skip sprint review.

Responding to change over following a plan

  • Never ending backlog grooming.
  • Split issues to tasks and tasks to sub-tasks. Divide and conquer.
  • Plan and Retrospect one sprint at a time.
  • Avoid Scope Creep
